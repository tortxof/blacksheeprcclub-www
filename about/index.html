--- layout: default title: About Us ---

Black Sheep RC Club has been providing a quality aero modeling experience since our founding in 1990.

We are located in Anderson, SC, not far from I-85.

We have a 40' by 300' asphalt runway, perfect for smaller electric models, as well as an 800' grass runway for large models.

Our goal as a club is to welcome new modelers to the hobby. We are happy to help new people get started.

Club meetings are held on the first tuesday of every month at 6:00 PM.
